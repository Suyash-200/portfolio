About Us

Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Suyash Srivastava,a frontend developer who believes every pixel has a purpose and every interaction should feel effortless.

Once upon a time, I began my journey writing code that simply worked. But over time — through real-world projects like CabKro and Coin Looty, and the challenge of turning static ideas into live, breathing interfaces — I discovered the joy of creating experiences, not just pages.

I specialize in HTML, CSS, Javascipt, React.js, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap and design systems that are both powerful and intuitive. With over 2 years of hands-on experience, I’ve built dashboards, portfolio sites, ERP modules, and components that not only look good — but work well across devices and users.

My tools are modern: React Icons, Chart.js, Vite, context APIs — but my goal is timeless: create digital experiences that are clean, fast, and human-centered.

Let’s turn vision into interaction, one smooth UI at a time.
