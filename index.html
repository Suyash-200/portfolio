About Us

Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Suyash Srivastava, a Frontend Developer with 2 years of full-time experience and 6 months of Freelance experience in creating responsive, mobile-friendly web applications using React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS and Bootstrap. Skilled in REST API integration, state management (Redux, Context API), and performance optimization, with a proven ability to translate Figma designs into production-ready applications.

Once upon a time, I began my journey writing code that simply worked. But over time — through real-world projects like Asset Management Software, ERP Software, and Family Hospital Website, and the challenge of turning static ideas into live, breathing interfaces — I discovered the joy of creating experiences, not just pages.

I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, Redux, and Context API for building interfaces that are both powerful and intuitive. With over 2 years of hands-on experience, I’ve built dashboards, ERP modules, hospital websites, and components that not only look good — but work well across devices and users.

My tools are modern: React, Redux, Context API, TypeScript, Vite — but my goal is timeless: create digital experiences that are clean, fast, and human-centered. I'm a immediate joiner and continuously upgrading skills by working on modern projects in TypeScript.

Let’s turn vision into interaction, one smooth UI at a time.
